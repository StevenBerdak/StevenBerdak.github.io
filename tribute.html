Stephan Hawking's Life

  • 1942 - Stephen Hawking was born in Oxford, the child of Franks and Eileen Hawking on January 8th.
  • 1959 - He began attending University College in Oxford. He was 17 years old.
  • 1966 - Hawkings thesis on the creation of the univserse was approved and Hawking received a research fellowship at Gonville and Caius College.
  • 1973 - Hawking began to study quantum gravity and quantum mechanics which would spur his later work in showing that black holes emit radiation.
  • 1975 - After returning to cambridge he was awarded the Eddington Medal as well as the Plus XI Gold Medal and was later appointed a chair in gravitational physics.
  • 1984 - Hawking completes the first draft of his book A Brief History of Time with the assistance of a speech-generating device.
  • 1992 - A film version of A Brief History of Time was released which also served as a biography for Hawking, much to his annoyance.
  • 2005 - Hawking releases a controversial solution to explain the 'information paradox' of black holes that theorized that black holes may have more than one topology.
  • 2018 - Hawking passes away at the age of 76 with the world mourning his death and celebrating his life.
